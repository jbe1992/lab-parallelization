Karen Clarke (born 1 February 1972) is an Australian netball player. Clarke formerly played with the Queensland Firebirds during the Commonwealth Bank Trophy.[1] Clarke also played in New Zealand's National Bank Cup in 2005, playing for the Canterbury Flames.[2] She retired from the Firebirds after the 2007 season and has since become a firefighter with the Queensland Fire and Rescue Service.[3]
